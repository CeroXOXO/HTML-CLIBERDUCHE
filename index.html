PROJECTS INFORMATION

CLIBERDUCHE CORPORATION has a variety of projects - from small, medium to large commercial and industrial projects.

The company specializes in the supply backfilling materials, aggregates and other land resources that the client requires.

The company also engages in the land development which includes clearing, cutting and peeling, levelling and RCP, PVC pipe laying. It has been involved in building bridges, concrete roads, gutter, ripraps, easement, and slope protection that includes erosion prevention and liquefaction of soil.
